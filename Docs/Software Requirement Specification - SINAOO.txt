1. Introduction

Purpose
      This document speci?es software requirements for an online learning platform based on web application written in JavaScript utilizing the MERN technological stack. The purpose of this document is to further describe the requirements and guide the development of the product.
      This product aims to introduce students to a highly gami?ed online learning platform and enhance their performance and engagement in a self-learning scenario determined by the quantitative and qualitative metrics, which are:
a. Quantitatively based on test grade and completion rate.
b. Qualitatively based on their experience after using the digital product through a survey.

Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions
      This product is targeted towards people who will partake in the national college admission test (SNBT). This product serves as a highly gami?ed online learning platform. Project Scope
      This product will be deployed as a web-application platform accessible through a browser, suiting both desktop and mobile devices, aiming to deliver the same experience beyond platforms. This product aims to cater to the needs of high school students and people who are going through a semi/gap year as a supplementary learning platform augmented with gami?cation elements, to create a gameful experience in a self-learning setting, enhancing learners' engagement, motivation, and performance. This product will only go through a single development and will not be further developed after the evaluation of the product.

3. System Features
For the application to serve its purpose, some products must be developed. These are some minimum viable products (MVP).

Course

Description and Priority
The course is the most important feature with high priority to be implemented in the application. This feature is the main feature for users to use the application by implementing its child; module, learning material, and quiz.

Stimulus/Responses Sequences
The User could choose a course and access its module to read learning material and practice the quiz testing their understanding of the module. Upon starting a new course, a new progress bar will be triggered to track completion Upon completion score will accumulate into points.

Functional Requirements

C-FR-003Points system integration
Module

Description and Priority
The module is the most important feature with high priority to be implemented in the application.

Stimulus/Responses Sequences
The User could choose a module to read learning material and do its quizzes. Upon opening a new module, a new progress bar will be triggered to track completion. Upon completion score will accumulate into points.

Points

Description and Priority
This feature is the main gami?cation element feature for users as the main purpose of the product. Points will be accumulated from progression (?nishing course, quiz scores in modules, and achievements). Accumulated points determine the placement of the user in the leaderboard and title.

Stimulus/Responses Sequences
Upon completion of a module, score of quiz, and reaching milestones, user will acquire points that will be accumulated into placement in the leaderboard and title.
